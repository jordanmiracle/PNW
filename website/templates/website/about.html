A look at what we do

24/7 Emergency Services
360-393-6189

We offer 24/7 emergency services because we know that not all emergencies happen during typical business hours. Whether a tree has fallen from a storm, an uprooted tree has ripped up piping, or anything else that constitutes an emergency, we are here. Serving Whatcom, Skagit, and Island Counties.

Storm Damage cleanup

Attempting to clean up weather damage on one's own can be incredibly dangerous. The risks of injury, are nearly as high after a storm as they are during it. Stray power lines, shattered glass, and debris such as splintered wood are just a few of the things that must be accounted for after a storm, and are assessed by our team. All of these cleanup procedures should be conducted by professionals, for your safety, and for the safety of your property. We diligently abide by ISA standards, and will work with you to ensure zero additional damage to your property.

Tree Removal

Trees are a beautiful and essential part of our world, and of your property, we all love them. However, we recognize when a tree is beyond recovery. This can happen for a variety of factors such as age, disease, storm damage. When this happens, and the tree is past the point of being able to heal, that is when we recommend removing the tree entirely. Dead trees are incapable of supporting themselves, and can lead to unnecessary hazards.

All this being said, it can be exceedingly dangerous to try and remove these trees, or even trim them on oneâ€™s own. When these issues arise, we are here to help. Your safety, and the safety of your property is of the utmost importance to our team. Our clients depend on us knowing how to safely remove trees that are damaged or diseased, and best-practices are always observed.

Stump Grinding / Removal

Once a tree is removed, the stump can remain and it will continue to grow, as will the roots. Stumps in and of themselves are not harmful. However, they can often get in the way of planting new trees, gardens, or getting in the way of construction projects. Eventually, it is also possible for the roots to begin to push up foundations or fencing. They also can be a haven for termites.

This is where we come in. After removing a tree, our team can remove the stump in the most effective way possible. Removing the stump of an old tree, in particular, can be incredibly time-consuming and challenging without the proper knowledge and equipment. We are able to grind down stumps in a quick and efficient manner.
